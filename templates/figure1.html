Mapping of average taxi fare per trip versus pick-up location for New York City yellow cab in January 2015. Color scale indicates average amount of taxi fare per trip: fare increases from dark blue to red and yellow. Average fare above $24 is plotted in yellow.

This figure clears shows that average taxi fare is strongly correlated with pick-up location.
